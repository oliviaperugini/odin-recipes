Sweet Potato Casserole

"My dream is to become a farmer. Just a Bohemian guy pulling up his own sweet potatoes for dinner." - Lenny Kravitz

Ingredients:

  • 5 large sweet potatoes
  • brown sugar
  • cinnamon
  • small and large marshmallows
  • olive oil
  • salt and pepper
  • and more!

Steps:

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 400 degrees.
  2. Peel the sweet potatoes and chop into cubes. Boil until tender.
  3. Mash the sweet potatoes and add brown sugar and cinnamon.
  4. Place sweet potato mixture into a casserole dish. Place in oven for 20 minutes.
  5. Take dish out of oven. Carefully (it's hot!), add the marshmallows on top.
  6. Cook an additional 10 minutes in the oven. If the marshmallows start turning dark brown, take the dish out early. Enjoy!
