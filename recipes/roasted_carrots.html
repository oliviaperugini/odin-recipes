Roasted Carrots

"If carrots got you drunk, rabbits would be messed-up." - Mitch Hedberg

Whether it's Thanksgiving or Easter Sunday, look no further for the perfect carrot side dish.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb. bag of large carrots
  • honey
  • balsamic vinegar
  • olive oil
  • salt and pepper

Steps:

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 400 degrees.
  2. Wash the carrots. (No cutting needed!)
  3. Mix the carrots with all the honey, balsamic vinegar, and olive oil. Once mixed, sprinkle salt and pepper on top.
  4. Lay flat on greased baking sheet.
  5. Cook in the oven for 20 minutes. Add additional time if carrots are extra thick. Enjoy!
